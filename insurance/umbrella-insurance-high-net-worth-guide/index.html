Umbrella Insurance for High-Net-Worth Households

How umbrella liability insurance can protect wealth, income, and high-value assets.

What umbrella insurance does

Umbrella insurance can provide additional liability protection above underlying home, auto, or other policies.

Why high-net-worth households care

Higher assets can create larger liability exposure. Umbrella coverage may help protect savings, investments, real estate equity, and future income.

Coverage considerations

Review limits, exclusions, underlying policy requirements, household drivers, rental properties, and recreational assets.

Professional review

A qualified insurance advisor can help coordinate umbrella coverage with home, auto, boat, and specialty policies.
