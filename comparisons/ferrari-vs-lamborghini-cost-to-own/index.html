Ferrari vs Lamborghini Cost to Own

Overview

Both brands can be expensive to own, but cost profiles vary by model, age, mileage, and usage.

Key cost factors

The most important costs usually include purchase price, financing, insurance, maintenance, taxes, storage, and the opportunity cost of tying up capital.

Risk considerations

Luxury asset decisions can involve depreciation, liquidity limits, market cycles, repair uncertainty, and changing interest rates. Conservative estimates are safer than optimistic ones.

How to compare options

Compare total cost, not just monthly payment. Review insurance quotes, financing terms, ongoing expenses, resale value, and how the purchase fits your broader financial picture.
